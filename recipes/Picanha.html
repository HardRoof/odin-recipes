Picanha

Description

Picanha (pronounced pee-kahn-yah) is a cut of beef very popular in Brazil. The closest cut in America is a Top Sirloin Cap with the fat cap left on. The large fat cap brings tremendous flavor and helps keep the steak juicy and flavorful.

Ingredients

  • 2-3lb Top Sirloin Cap with the fat cap left on
  • 4 Tablespoons fleur de sel

Instructions

  1. Cut the picanha in 3-4 cm slices and salt the pieces with fleur de sel
  2. Grill it (Preferable on charcoal)
  3. Wait 1-3 min to ensure all the fat is reabsorbed into the meat, slice and serve it
